Lifeforger

Backstory

Lifeforger, a gifted healer, was born with a rare talent for restoring life's essence. Growing up among healers, their natural affinity for the mystical arts became evident. Lifeforger's reputation soared as tales spread of their ability to summon healing drones, mend injuries, and revitalize weary warriors. Their most remarkable feat was the creation of the Care Package, a chest filled with life-infused energy and high-tier resources. Lifeforger became a beacon of hope, revered for their boundless compassion and unmatched mastery of healing.
