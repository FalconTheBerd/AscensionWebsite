Fade

Backstory

Fade, an enigmatic master of holographic technology, dwells in the twilight between light and shadow. With his expertise, he manipulates quantum energy to create illusions so convincing, they deceive even the sharpest senses. Neither wholly good nor completely evil, Fade's neutral stance allows him to navigate the world with a mystique accentuated by his mesmerizing holographic creations.

Tactical Ability: Decoy

Release a decoy that will walk in a straight line.

Ultimate Ability: Quantum Barrage

Fade channels quantum energy, unleashing a rapid-fire barrage of energy projectiles.
