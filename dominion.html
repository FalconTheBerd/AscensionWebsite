Dominion

Backstory

Dominion, a malevolent strategist with an uncanny ability to track his prey, delighted in manipulating shadows and orchestrating doom. His sinister intellect and remorseless cruelty made him a formidable force, striking fear into the hearts of even the bravest warriors. With his eerie presence and unparalleled tracking abilities, he became a harbinger of despair, leaving a trail of darkness in his wake.

Tactical Ability: Marked for Death

See enemies through walls for a short amount of time.

Ultimate Ability: Finish Them

Speed up and see more enemies through walls for longer.
