Bullseye

Backstory

Bullseye, the mysterious assassin, is renowned for his unmatched accuracy and ability to eliminate targets silently. Operating in the shadows, he marks and tracks his victims with deadly precision, earning him the name "The Masked Assassin." Motivated by a twisted sense of justice, he will kill anyone who gets in his way.

Tactical Ability: Marked

Receive a Marking Bow and 3 arrows, the last person hit by the Marking Bow will be tagged. Left clicking with the bow will bring up a menu allowing you to find and damage the tagged player.

Ultimate Ability: Marked for Death

Kill your current tagged player and mark anyone surrounding them.
